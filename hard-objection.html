Hard-objection analysis

Why Russell without tobacco-industry tenure?

The strongest credible objection is not about process methodology. It is domain proximity and global enterprise scale.

The Objection, Plainly

PMI could hire a business-process leader who already understands tobacco or nicotine regulation, a global consumer-goods matrix, the company’s market structures, internal systems, and the specific process language of the organization. Russell would have a real ramp.

What Not to Claim

Do not claim direct nicotine expertise

Adjacent cannabis-tech operations, regulated drone operations, manufacturing, and chemistry grounding do not equal tobacco-domain mastery.

Do not claim PMI-scale transformation ownership

Amazon and Compunetics evidence demonstrates scale and complexity; it should not be rewritten as ownership of a comparable global enterprise-transformation portfolio.
