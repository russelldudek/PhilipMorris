120-day entry plan

Earn the right to standardize.

A senior process-excellence leader should start with process truth, then build proof and governance.

Days 1–30

Decode

Map stakeholders, process ownership, and two representative end-to-end pilots. Baseline outcome, flow, control, economics, and adoption.

Days 31–60

Make variation explicit

Create Process Genome maps. Separate required variation from unmanaged debt. Clarify decision rights, owners, controls, and review dates.

Days 61–90

Run targeted trials

Test a small number of interventions against explicit hypotheses. Measure business result, flow, risk, economics, and adoption.

Days 91–120

Institutionalize learning

Establish portfolio governance, process-owner cadence, benefit validation, and scaling rules based on what pilots proved.
