Mandate Hypothesis

Build an enterprise mechanism that turns cross-functional process friction into a governed portfolio of redesign interventions, separates necessary local variation from process debt, clarifies end-to-end ownership and decision rights, and proves whether redesigned work creates sustained business value.

Company Moment

Portfolio transformation

PMI reports that its smoke-free business generated 43% of total global net revenues in Q1 2026, with more than 43 million estimated legal-age consumers across 108 markets.

Transformation system

PMI describes its business transformation as powered by science, data, digital innovation, human judgment, and responsible decision-making. Scale therefore depends on disciplined process learning and adoption.

Five Operating Tensions

Global vs local

Standardize enough to scale and compare without flattening regulatory and market reality.

Speed vs traceability

Transform quickly while preserving clear control logic and evidence.

Ambition vs capacity

Prioritize interventions against finite organizational change bandwidth.

Visibility vs adoption

Data can expose friction; routines and role design determine whether behavior changes.

End-to-end vs function

Give process outcomes real ownership without creating a parallel bureaucracy.

White-Space Thesis

Business Process Excellence should behave like a Process Genome: define the stable core, make legitimate variants explicit, detect harmful mutations, and evolve standards from evidence.

Strongest Objection

Concern: Russell has no direct tobacco/nicotine background. Response: acknowledge the gap, then argue adjacent evidence: direct cross-functional process transformation, manufacturing and quality systems, high-reliability operations, regulated aviation, global technical stakeholders, 24/7 logistics, and AI-enabled workflow design. Domain learning comes first; transferability is in the operating mechanisms.
